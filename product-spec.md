# Product Specification: Dinosaur warrior

A robotic T-Rex toy featuring motorized back-mounted cannons that deploy and fire soft foam projectiles, complete with LED eyes and sound effects.

**Category:** Interactive Mechanical Toy  
**Target Unit Cost:** $25 USD  
**Dimensions:** 120×220×300 mm  

## Power

- Source: 18650 Li-Ion Cell
- Voltage: 3.7V nominal (boosted to 5V for servos/audio)
- Capacity: 2500 mAh

## Firmware Features

- Servo state machine
- I2S Audio playback
- LED animation sequencing
- GPIO Interrupt (Button Trigger)

## Electronics

- **MCU:** ESP32-C3
- **Connectivity:** BLE, WiFi
